# Subscription & Payment Terms

**Effective date:** 1 May 2026
**Last updated:** 1 May 2026

This document sets out the financial terms on which paid plans of
the Service are provided. It is incorporated into the [Terms of
Use](./terms-of-use.md). The [Refund & Cancellation Policy](./refund-cancellation-policy.md)
controls all refund and cancellation matters. Capitalised terms
have the meanings given in the Terms of Use.

---

## 1. Plans

The Service is offered on the following tiers:

| Plan | How to start | Notes |
|------|--------------|-------|
| **Free** | Sign up at https://nanoasm.com | No payment method required. Capped resource limits. May be modified or withdrawn at any time. |
| **Starter** | Self-serve via Settings → Billing | Paid monthly or annually. |
| **Professional** | Self-serve via Settings → Billing | Paid monthly or annually. |
| **Enterprise Silver** | Self-serve via Settings → Billing | Paid monthly or annually. |
| **Enterprise Gold** | Contact sales — https://nanoasm.com/#contact | Custom contract, custom pricing, no public self-serve checkout. |

Plan limits (assets, scans/month, monitored assets, scheduled scans,
team members, API keys, monitoring frequency, deep discovery,
webhooks) are published in the Service and may be adjusted with
reasonable notice as set out in §6.

## 2. Fees and currency

- The fees for each paid plan are displayed on https://nanoasm.com
  and inside the Service at the point of subscribing. The fees
  shown at the time you subscribe are the fees you agree to.
- Fees are quoted and charged in **United States dollars (USD)**
  unless explicitly stated otherwise.
- Your bank or card issuer may apply currency-conversion fees if
  you are billed in a currency different from your card's local
  currency. We do not control or refund those fees.

## 3. Billing cycles

You may choose **monthly** or **annual** billing for any paid tier
(except Enterprise Gold, which is governed by your individual
contract).

- **Monthly** — billed once per calendar month on the day you
  subscribed (or the closest valid date). Renews automatically at
  the end of each month unless cancelled.
- **Annual** — billed once for twelve months in advance. Renews
  automatically at the end of each twelve-month period unless
  cancelled. Annual subscriptions are typically priced lower per
  month than monthly equivalents.

## 4. Payment method

To start a paid subscription you must provide a valid payment
method. We use **Stripe** as our payment processor. **We do not
collect, see, or store your card number** — Stripe collects payment
details directly through its hosted checkout, retains them, and
processes charges on our behalf.

You authorise us (acting through Stripe) to charge your payment
method:

- the initial subscription fee at the point of subscribing,
- the renewal fee at the start of each subsequent billing period,
- any prorated charges arising from plan changes (see §7),
- applicable taxes (see §5).

It is your responsibility to keep your payment method up to date.
Update it via *Manage billing* in Settings → Billing.

## 5. Taxes

Prices displayed on https://nanoasm.com and inside the Service are
**exclusive of any applicable tax** unless explicitly stated.

Where required by law, the applicable tax will be added at checkout
and on each renewal invoice. We use Stripe Tax (where enabled) to
determine and collect the correct amount based on your billing
address. Examples of taxes that may apply:

- **Australia** — Goods and Services Tax (GST) at 10% for
  Australian customers.
- **United Kingdom and European Union** — value-added tax (VAT) at
  the rate applicable in your country.
- **United States** — sales tax in jurisdictions where we have
  nexus.

If you have a valid tax identification number (e.g. an Australian
ABN, an EU VAT number, or a UK VAT number), enter it during
checkout — Stripe will apply reverse-charge or zero-rate where
applicable, in accordance with the rules of your jurisdiction.

You are responsible for:

- providing accurate billing-address and tax-ID information,
- any withholding tax that you are required to withhold and remit
  in your jurisdiction.

## 6. Price changes

We may change the price of a paid plan from time to time. If we do:

- **For your current subscription** — the new price will not take
  effect until your next renewal. You will receive at least
  **30 days' notice** before the renewal at the new price. If you
  do not wish to renew at the new price, cancel before the renewal
  date.
- **For new subscribers** — the new price applies immediately upon
  publication.
- **For introductory or promotional pricing** — once any
  introductory period ends, your subscription continues at the
  then-current standard price for your plan and cycle.

Promotional discounts (where offered) are subject to their own
terms at the time of redemption and do not modify these
Subscription & Payment Terms.

## 7. Plan changes mid-cycle

You can change plans at any time via *Manage billing* in Settings →
Billing. When you switch:

- Stripe automatically credits the **unused portion** of your
  previous plan against your new plan,
- you are charged the **prorated cost** of the new plan for the
  remainder of the current billing period,
- the next renewal is at the new plan's full rate and on the new
  plan's cycle.

Plan changes are not refunds — see the [Refund & Cancellation
Policy](./refund-cancellation-policy.md) §3.

## 8. Auto-renewal

**Subscriptions renew automatically** at the end of each billing
period (monthly or annual) until you cancel.

We will:

- charge the renewal fee on or shortly before the renewal date,
- email you a receipt for each successful payment from
  **no-reply@nanoasm.com**,
- show your current period end date and any pending cancellation in
  Settings → Billing.

We will **not** typically send a reminder before each automatic
renewal of a monthly subscription. For annual subscriptions, we
will send a renewal reminder a reasonable time in advance.

## 9. Failed payment

If we cannot charge your payment method on a renewal:

1. Your subscription enters a **past-due** state.
2. We email your billing contact and display a banner inside the
   Service.
3. Stripe automatically retries the payment over approximately
   three weeks.
4. You retain access to paid features during the retry window so
   you have time to resolve the issue.
5. If all retries fail, the subscription is **automatically
   cancelled** and your Organisation is downgraded to the Free
   plan.

Persistent non-payment may result in suspension or termination of
the Account in addition to subscription cancellation.

## 10. Trials

Trials of paid plans are **not automatic** and do **not** start
through the public checkout flow. They are **request-only**:

- A user clicks *Request free trial* on the relevant plan card.
- We review the request manually and decide whether to grant a
  trial and for how long.
- If granted, the requested plan is enabled on the Organisation
  for a defined period at no charge.
- No payment method is required during the trial period and no
  charge is taken until and unless the user converts to a paid
  plan.

If the trial period ends without conversion, the Organisation
reverts to the Free plan automatically.

If the user converts to a paid plan during or at the end of the
trial, the standard rules in this document apply from the
conversion date forward. The trial period is **not** retroactively
billed.

## 11. Enterprise Gold and custom contracts

Enterprise Gold is **not available through self-serve checkout**.
Pricing, limits, and contract terms are negotiated individually,
typically including:

- annual or multi-year commitment,
- agreed usage caps and overage handling,
- a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) and any required addenda,
- service-level commitments (SLA exhibit),
- custom invoicing terms (e.g. NET-30 payment via Stripe Invoicing
  or bank transfer rather than card on file).

Where a written agreement exists between you and us in respect of
Enterprise Gold (or any custom contract), the terms of that
agreement control over this document for any conflicting provision.

## 12. Cancellation

Cancellation is governed by the [Refund & Cancellation
Policy](./refund-cancellation-policy.md). In summary:

- You can cancel at any time from *Manage billing* in Settings →
  Billing.
- Your subscription stays active until the end of the **current
  paid billing period** — cancellation does not entitle you to a
  refund of unused time, except as provided in the Refund &
  Cancellation Policy.
- After the period ends, your Organisation is downgraded to the
  Free plan.

## 13. Refunds

The full refund position is set out in the [Refund & Cancellation
Policy](./refund-cancellation-policy.md). In short:

- Subscription fees are **non-refundable for elapsed and unused
  time** in a period you have already paid for, except in the
  exceptions listed in that policy.
- Refunds are issued back to the original payment method via
  Stripe and typically appear within 5–10 business days.

## 14. Invoices and receipts

For every successful subscription payment we email a branded receipt
from **no-reply@nanoasm.com** to the billing contact on file. The
receipt shows the line items (including any proration), subtotal,
applicable tax, and total paid, and includes a link to the
Stripe-hosted invoice PDF.

Stripe-hosted invoices satisfy invoice-format requirements in most
jurisdictions, including EU/UK B2B requirements where the buyer's
billing address and any provided VAT number are on the invoice.

You can access past invoices and download PDFs at any time via
*Manage billing* in Settings → Billing.
