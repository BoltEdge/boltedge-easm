# Skill: competitor-pulse

**Owner agent:** strategy (Ava)
**Trigger:** scheduled (Tuesday 08:00 founder timezone) or manual
**Output:** markdown email digest sent to the founder

## Inputs
- web_search to identify current top competitors in ASM/EASM
- web_fetch on each competitor's product/pricing/blog pages

## Steps
1. Identify 3–5 most relevant competitors.
2. Fetch their public pages; note what changed in the last ~30 days.
3. Surface themes and flag director-worthy moves.
4. Email the digest.

## Voice
Direct, market-aware, evidence-backed. Cite URLs. Never invent competitor activity.
